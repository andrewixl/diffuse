How to Play the Game

This game is a best of five rounds. Each round will begin with the good guys receiving a digit that will be part of the code. The good guys will attempt to convince the diffuser that their digit is the correct one, while the bad guy will attempt to convince the diffuser that his fake digit is a part of the code. The diffuser will then decide which digits to put in and if the bad guy manages to trick the diffuser the bad guy wins.
